About Us

Clean it up, founded by Trampas Bowers in 2009, provides professional cleaning services to homes and businesses throughout Blountville, Piney Flats, Johnson City, Kingsport, Gray, Jonesboro, Telford, Elizabethton, Roan Mountain, and Butler.
We are fully licensed, insured, and bonded for your peace of mind.
We do residential homes of any size, commercial cleaning, and Airbnbs. We also have several medical offices, dentists, and chiropractors.
