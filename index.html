About Us

Clean it up provides professional cleaning services for homes, businesses, and Airbnbs throughout Northeast Tennessee. Our service area includes Blountville, Johnson City, Kingsport, Piney Flats, Gray, Jonesborough, Telford, Elizabethton, Roan Mountain, and Butler. Founded in 2009, we are licensed, bonded, and insured. We offer FREE estimates, so contact us today to discuss your cleaning needs. Call Now for a FREE Estimate

Services

  • Our cleaning professionals are certified in deep cleaning techniques, ensuring a thorough and comprehensive clean.
  • We offer comprehensive spring cleaning services to refresh and revitalize your space.
  • We provide flexible scheduling options with weekly and bi-weekly cleaning visits to maintain a consistently clean environment.

We proudly serve the Northeast Tennessee area, including Blountville, Piney Flats, Johnson City, Kingsport, Gray, Jonesborough, Telford, Elizabethton, Roan Mountain, and Butler. We handle cleaning jobs of all sizes, from individual homes and apartments to businesses and rental properties including Airbnbs.
